  - 2 AI/physics specialists
  - 1 balance design expert

## Market Competitiveness

### Unique Value Propositions
1. **Dynamic Resource Management**: Sophisticated ammo and weapon systems
2. **Adaptive Difficulty**: Exponential scaling with strategic depth
3. **Varied Enemy Interactions**: Complex physics and AI behaviors

### Competitive Differentiation
- Advanced weapon ammo tracking
- Nuanced enemy interaction mechanics
- Depth beyond traditional arcade shooters

## Risk Identification

### High-Risk Areas
1. **Complexity Barrier**: Potential alienation of existing player base
2. **Performance Challenges**: Technical limitations on mobile devices
3. **Balance Issues**: Exponential difficulty scaling may frustrate players

### Mitigation Strategies
- Implement difficulty modes (Casual/Standard/Expert)
- Develop comprehensive tutorial system
- Create adaptive performance scaling
- Extensive beta testing with existing community
